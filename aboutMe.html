About Me

Welcome to my website! I'm Ralph, and I'm delighted to have you here. Allow me to introduce myself.

I’m a 25-year-old robotics engineer with passion for designing dependable intelligent machines that leverage technology to further efficiency. During 4 years of experience in the field, including 2 years of research in Singapore, I've grabbed every opportunity I can to explore many facets including manufacturing, UAVs/UGVs, legged robots, and automotive engineering. I graduated from the University of Leeds with a Master’s in Mechatronics and Robotics in 2021, and throughout my journey, I've been driven by relentless curiosity and a mission to create reliable and accurate working partners in every aspect of life. I believe in the transformative power of robotics and its potential to reshape industries and improve lives, allowing people to focus on the more creative and fulfilling aspects of design, engineering, and everyday life.

During my time in Singapore, I’ve had the incredible opportunity to work on novel aerial robotics alongside experienced researchers and engineers at the Singapore University of Technology and Design. This broadened my horizons and deepened my understanding of the field, allowing me explore drone swarming, SLAM, and robot-robot collaboration. It exposed me to diverse perspectives and approaches, fostering a global mindset that continues to shape my work today. What sets me apart is my constant enthusiasm for learning new technologies, which can be seen through the broad array of projects and roles I showcase here. By combining my technical expertise with a deep understanding of human-machine interaction, I strive to develop robots that are not only functional but also robust and intuitive for other engineers.

When I'm not immersed in the world of robotics, you can find me maintaining my project car, indulging in the exhilarating world of sim racing, or following McLaren in Formula 1. These hobbies provide me with a much-needed balance and allow me to explore my creativity outside of my professional pursuits. I find that they fuel my problem-solving skills, as well as my ability to think on my feet and adapt to dynamic situations.

This website serves as a platform for me to share my knowledge, insights, and projects with you. I hope you find valuable resources, inspiration, and a glimpse into the fascinating world of robotics engineering. Feel free to explore and connect with me through my LinkedIn at the bottom of the page. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
